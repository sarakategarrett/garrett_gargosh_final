About to DIY Money

DIY Money was founded in 2022 by two professors. The main idea of DIY Money is to help educate those that want to DIY thier own finances. The two main puposes for this site is to provide educaiton resources and a software system to help manage the wealth.




Meet Dr. Peter Cardon

Dr. Cardon is a DIY enthusiast. He has many accomplishments. A recent one graduating from the University of Georgia with a Master's degree in Personal Financial Planning. Dr. Cardon is an expert in communication and has one of the most popular textbooks in business communication on the market. Dr. Cardon is an avid researcher. He has many citations and has presented in conferences all over the world. Dr. Cardon is also an avid runner. He has completed marathons and half marathons for fun. Many of the ideas posted in the resource section are from Dr. Cardon.




Meet Dr. Bryan Marshall

Dr. Marshall currently teaches computing at Georgia College. Like Dr. Cardon, he recently graduated from the University of Georgia with a Master's degree in Personal Financial Planning. His PhD is in Management Information Systems from Utah State University. Dr. Marshall served in the United States Marine Corps for 6 years in the infantry (0311). In his off time he loves being with his kids and traveling the US.
